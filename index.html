Meet Dr. Koch

A flight surgeon who never stopped flying.

Jim Koch isn't a physician who happens to sign off on pilots. He's an ATP-rated pilot, a CFII and MEII flight instructor, an A&P/IA mechanic, and a retired United States Air Force flight surgeon.

That means when you sit down for your exam, you're talking to someone who understands your medical and your mission. No surprises, no runaround — just an examiner who knows exactly what's at stake when your certificate is on the line.
